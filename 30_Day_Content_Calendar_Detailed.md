## Content Format Guide

### Twitter/X Posts
- **Educational Threads:** 5-10 tweets, numbered, clear takeaways
- **Quick Tips:** Single tweet with value
- **Engagement Posts:** Questions, polls, "tag a friend"
- **Promotional:** Clear value prop, link, social proof

### LinkedIn Content
- **Articles:** 500-800 words, professional tone, data-driven
- **Company Updates:** Milestones, hires, culture
- **Thought Leadership:** Industry trends, predictions

### Visual Content
- **Graphics:** Clean, branded, minimal text
- **Videos:** <60 seconds for social, 3-5 minutes for YouTube
- **Carousels:** Step-by-step guides, lists, comparisons

### Community Content
- **Discord:** Daily check-ins, help channels, off-topic chat
- **Reddit:** Value-first, no overt promotion
- **Newsletter:** Weekly curated content + original
